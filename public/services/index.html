Services

My work sits at the intersection of strategic intelligence, geopolitical risk, and information threats. I support private-sector and institutional clients with analysis designed for decision-making: executive briefings, risk assessments, stakeholder mapping, and structured monitoring of political, regulatory, and informational environments across France, Eastern Europe, and Russia.

Geopolitical & Political Risk Analysis

I provide political and geopolitical analysis for organisations exposed to complex or fast-moving environments. This includes assessment of political, regulatory, security, and institutional developments, with a focus on their operational and strategic implications.

Typical outputs include risk notes, briefing papers, scenario assessments, and decision-support memoranda tailored to a specific country, issue, or period. My work covers France, Central and Eastern Europe, Russia, Ukraine, and the MENA region.

Strategic Monitoring & Early Warning

I design and run structured monitoring systems for clients who need continuous visibility over political, regulatory, sectoral, or reputational developments. This includes tracking weak signals, identifying shifts in the operating environment, and translating large volumes of open-source information into concise analytical outputs.

This service is suited to organisations requiring regular strategic updates, executive summaries, or tailored reporting to support planning, market positioning, or institutional engagement.

Disinformation & FIMI Analysis

I analyse foreign information manipulation and interference, media narratives, and cross-platform amplification dynamics. My work in this area combines structured monitoring, narrative analysis, ecosystem mapping, and OSINT-based research to help clients understand how information threats evolve and how they affect public debate, institutions, and organisational exposure.

This service is relevant to public institutions, media actors, and organisations operating in contested informational environments or exposed to politically driven reputational and influence risks.

Stakeholder Mapping & Ecosystem Analysis

I support strategic positioning, market-entry decisions, and due diligence through stakeholder mapping and ecosystem analysis. This includes identifying key actors, institutional relationships, sector dynamics, and decision-making structures relevant to a client’s objectives.

Outputs are designed to clarify the distribution of influence, flag potential points of political or regulatory friction, and inform engagement strategy.

Strategic Communication in Sensitive Environments

I advise on communication and positioning where political sensitivity, media scrutiny, or reputational exposure require a more disciplined approach. This includes messaging support, narrative framing, media monitoring, and communication input informed by political and informational context.

This service builds on earlier work in public relations and external positioning, but remains closely tied to strategic analysis rather than general communications consulting.
