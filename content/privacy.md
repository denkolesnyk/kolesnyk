## Data collected

This site does not collect any personal data through forms. No third-party advertising or tracking cookies are used.

## Server logs

GitHub, Inc. and Cloudflare, Inc. automatically collect access logs (IP address, browser, pages visited, timestamp) as part of normal hosting and network operations. This processing is carried out on the basis of legitimate interests — specifically, ensuring the security, stability, and performance of the site. This data is not used for advertising purposes.

For more information, see [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement) and [Cloudflare's Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

## Performance measurement (Cloudflare Browser Insights)

This site uses Cloudflare Browser Insights to measure page load performance (LCP, INP, CLS). According to Cloudflare, these measurements are collected in aggregated form and no cookies are set for this purpose. Processing is carried out on the basis of legitimate interests in monitoring and improving site performance.

For more information, see [Cloudflare's Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

## Your rights

To the extent that personal data is processed in connection with this site, it is handled exclusively by Cloudflare, Inc. and GitHub, Inc. under their respective privacy policies, linked above. For any questions regarding the processing of your data by these providers, please refer directly to their privacy policies or data protection contacts.
